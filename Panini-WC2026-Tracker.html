❓ How to use this app

📦 Every time you pull a sticker from a pack, tap it once. First tap = you got it (turns green). Tap again if it's a double — it counts a spare!
ℹ️ Tap the little blue circle on a sticker to fix mistakes, add spares, or mark a ⭐ star.
🔤 Every sticker has a code printed on it, like ARG 10. Type it in the search box to jump straight there.
🌍 Stickers are grouped by team. Tap a team header to open it; the chip number matches the number on the sticker.

🏆 Smart collecting tips

🎯 The ~150 pack sweet spot. Buying about 150 packs (~3 boxes) fills most of your album. After that, new stickers get rare — stop buying packs and start trading instead.
🥤 Coca-Cola stickers. The 12 special Coca-Cola stickers are NOT in normal packs. Find them under the labels of specially marked Coca-Cola bottles.
🔄 Trade your doubles. Use the Trade tab. Show your QR to a friend or scan theirs to instantly see what you can swap.
⭐ Keep star stickers safe. Mark big players and shiny stickers with a star. One star sticker can often be traded for 5–10 regular ones.
🏁 The final 50. When you need 50 stickers or fewer, you can order the exact numbers from Panini's official Missing Sticker Service.
📱 Free option. The official FIFA Panini Collection app lets you build a digital album with free packs every day.
The album holds 980 stickers — the Panini emblem, 19 World Cup History stickers, and 48 national teams of 20 each — plus 12 Coca-Cola stickers (992 total). Each sticker has its own code, like ARG 10 (Argentina, sticker 10). Team names and codes follow the official Panini checklist.
